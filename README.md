# Trash-Collector-Game
Simple mini-game where your character must prevent the falling trash from hitting the ground. 
# Controls:
'A' to move left  
'D' to move right
# How the game works:
You control the bottom block (The big red square) and must stop the falling trash (currectly shown as a mini red square).
You must stop the falling trash from hitting the bottom of the screen by moving your character under the falling trash. Each piece of trash you collect, you will gain 1 point. As you collect more points, the harder the game gets. The falling trash speed will increases down the screen and the enemy will begin to throw a second piece of trash shortly after thev first piece.
